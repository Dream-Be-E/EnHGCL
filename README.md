## EnHGCL

Various types of similarity are integrated by HetEGCL, providing different insights into the biological mechanisms of MDA, LDA, and LMI.
A cross-layer fusion strategy for node-specific information is proposed, which can better utilize the information of nodes in multi-task prediction.
The relation-aware attention mechanism is introduced into the HIN2Vec framework, which effectively captures the topological structure information of nodes under multi-task relationships.
A graph augmentation construction strategy is proposed and combined with a contrastive learning mechanism to enhance the robustness of embedding vectors.
